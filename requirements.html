Requirements

  • A WordPress site, running version 4.7+.
  • PHP 5.6+, with the cURL extension.
  • Composer.
  • Selenium (the "Standalone Server" version).

Driver requirements

The WP-CLI driver requires WP-CLI (version 0.24.0 or newer), to be installed globally:

  curl -o wp https://raw.githubusercontent.com/wp-cli/builds/gh-pages/phar/wp-cli.phar
  chmod +x wp

The executable must be named wp and be within your system's $PATH.
